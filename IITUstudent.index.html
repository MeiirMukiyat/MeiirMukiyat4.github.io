What do we know about IITU?

MUIT is the only university in Kazakhstan that received the ASIIN institutional international accreditation in 2019 and passed the procedure of official confirmation of compliance with international standards. "The International University of Information Technology guarantees the fulfillment of institutional, procedural and cultural requirements for high-quality teaching and successful learning," experts from ASIIN (Agency for Accreditation of Educational Programs in Engineering, Computer Science, Natural Sciences and Mathematics, Germany) confirm. For 10 years of development, MUIT has been able to firmly enter the ranking of the best technical universities in the country. The University is constantly working to improve the quality of educational services, provides a high potential of the teaching staff. In December 2019, according to the rating of the National Chamber of Entrepreneurs "Atameken", MUIT entered the TOP 3 universities of Kazakhstan, as well as the first place in such educational programs
